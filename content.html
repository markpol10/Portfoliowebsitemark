Thank you for showing interest in my portfoliowebsite. On this website you will find a showcase of some of the work I have done over the past two years.

The front-end design for a new website

Also in my second year of college, I got the assignment to design a new website for the company "Bedrijfkracht". During this asignment I improved a lot in HTML, CSS and Javascript. Also during this assignment, I started working with the famework Bootstrap, which now is the main framework I use.

A new mobile app for our school

One of the assignments school gave me, was to design a new application for them. During this assignment, we had complete freedom in what it could look like. Our goal was to design it based on research we had to do on the needs of the students.

Creating a 360° VR environment

During the second year of my education, me and my projectgroup got the assignment to figure out how to improve the image of solarfarms. To achieve this goal, we decided to create a 360° VR environment which allows the users to visually see how a solarfarm would fit into their local area. This visual aspect was assisted with a voice-over and other positive information. Overall this project helped me improve my hard skills such as Photoshop and 360° imagery. And it also helped me with soft skills such as a good communication between stakeholders, considering that was a really important aspect for us.

Creating an interactive infographic and test

While in my second year of college, my projectgroup and I got the assignment to help people that are in danger of losing their job due to it becoming unnecessary because of improvement in technology. We decided to set up a campaign to raise awareness on this issue. This was done by creating an information filled infographic and a test to let them see what job directions fits them.
This project greatly helped me with my front-end abilities and even helped me a little bit with my back-end.
