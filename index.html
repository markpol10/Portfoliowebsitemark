Who am I?

Hello! My name is Mark van de Pol. I am a 23 year old currently studying Communication and multimedia design and am in my second year. I am majoring in Interaction design and I have inspirations to become front-end web developer. My interests range from designing websites and applications to user experience and UI. By combining this with my love for creative design, I am having a great time developing myself.

What do I do?

As I am focussing on becoming a front-end developer, I am really interested in turning my ideas in working products, like websites, and applications. The things I like to focus most on, is the overall visual experience the user has when using the products I develop. I ty to realize this by using my skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript.

The thing I like to do most, is to first express my ideas by visualizing them using either pencil and paper or by doing this directly in a tool like Adobe Experience design or Axure. To support the visualizing of my ideas, I use Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to make the images and illustrations that I use.

After this, I really enjoy turning my prototypes into a working product. This is something I do using multiple tools. The code-editor that mainly use, is Sublime text. For most of the projects where I work with other people, I use Github to support the code editing. Also I recently started using Wordpress to help develop certain products.

Some of the work I have done

The front-end design for a new website

In my second year of college, I got the assignment to design a new website for the company "Bedrijfkracht". During this asignment I improved a lot in HTML, CSS and Javascript. Also during this assignment, I started working with the famework Bootstrap, which now is the main framework I use.

A new mobile app for our school

One of the assignments school gave me, was to design a new application for them.During this assignment, we had complete freedom in what it could look like. Our goal was to design it based on research we had to do on the needs of the students.

Creating a 360째 VR environment

During the second year of my education, me and my projectgroup got the assignment to figure out how to improve the image of solarfarms. To achieve this goal, we decided to create a 360째 VR environment which allows the users to visually see how a solarfarm would fit into their local area.

Creating an interactive infographic and test

While in my second year of college, my projectgroup and I got the assignment to help people that are in danger of losing their job due to it becoming unnecessary because of improvement in technology. We decided to set up a campaign to raise awareness on this issue. This was done by creating an information filled infographic and a quiz.

Small documentary about poverty in the Netherlands

Last year, my project group and I got the opportunity to make a small video for a local tv/radio channel here in Groningen. We decided to try and make a small documentary. We ended up with the decision to focus on poverty and specifically the local food bank. It focusses on the personal story of a person that makes use of the food bank.
